About us

"The Bookshelf"

Old, little-known books are rarely found in official stores. And new books can often be out of our budget. Also, probably all of us have a desire to exchange read and abandoned books for others, new and interesting works for us, we all want to renew our shelves, but I found that in Georgia there was no mobile or web platform where we could carry out these operations, so, there was no website for books For lovers! This motivated us to create "The Bookshelf"!
